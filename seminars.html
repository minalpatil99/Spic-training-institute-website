Seminar on Career trends in manfucturing Industry

An interactive session was held at EDC building on 27th March,2018 for the Engineering students on "Career trends in manfucturing Industry" . The session was to give insight to the students about the current scenario of career opportunities for IT graduate in Manufacturing Industry.

The session was delivered by Ms. Arpita Gupta MD Kadkraft who is B.Tech from NIT Kurukshetra and M.S. from BITS Pilani.SHe has close to 29 years of experience in IT industry. For past 27 years She is running an IT company - Kadkraft Systems Pvt. Ltd. In Chandigarh. They provide design and manufacturing simulation solutions to automotive and industrial machinery customers all over India. The customer includes companies like Maruti, Mahindra, Escorts, Bajaj Auto, Daimler, Tata Motors, Ashok Leyland, Royal Enfield, L&T and HAL. The session was attended by final semester students of B.E CSC, IT & Mechnical who are persuing their Industrial Training program at SPIC.
